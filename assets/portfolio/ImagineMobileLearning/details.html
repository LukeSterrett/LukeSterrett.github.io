Imagine Mobile Learning
About

Enriched with a wide array of features, this app lets you deploy the training courses which can be purchased and integrated by the trainees. It also serves as a one-stop destination for developing training programs and assessment tests, thus making it a full-fledged human resource training tool. By delivering a variety of options in courses to choose from, preview features, user reviews, free-of-cost trial packages and a convenient purchase process, etc.
